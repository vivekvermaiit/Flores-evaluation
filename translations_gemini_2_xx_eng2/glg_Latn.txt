This new environment has different resources and different competition, so the new population will need different characteristics or adaptations to be a stronger competitor than it needed to be until now.
The original population hasn't changed at all; they still require the same adaptations as before.
As time passes, when the new population begins to adapt to its new environment, it also starts to look less and less like the rest of the population.
Finally, after thousands or even millions of years, the two populations will have such a different appearance that they can no longer be considered the same species.
