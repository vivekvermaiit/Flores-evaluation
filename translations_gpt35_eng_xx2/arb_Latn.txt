Al-abwaab munaasilat abshaaree masarriyeh wa al-azmaan yeustaamiloona fi mozaakarateeheem laasm al-usrah aw bil asmaa alwazifiyah.
Al-wasat fi al-mahal al-'amali muhim, mubadir 'ala jihd al-jama'a ba'da madh al-najahat al-fardiya.
