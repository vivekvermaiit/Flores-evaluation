This new environment has different resources and different competitors, so the new population must have different characteristics or adapt to be a stronger competitor than it needed to be before.
The original population has not changed at all, they still need the same adaptations.
Over time, as the new population begins to adapt to the new environment, it will resemble the other population less and less.
In the end, after thousands or even millions of years, the two populations will differ so much from each other that they can no longer be called the same species.
