In this new environment, there are different resources and different competitors, so the new population will need traits or adaptations different from those required in the past to be a strong competitor.
The original population has not changed at all, they still need the same accommodations as in the past.
Over time, as the new population begins to adapt to its new environment, they start to look less and less like the other population.
