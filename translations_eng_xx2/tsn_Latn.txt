Tirisanommogo mo lefelong la tiro e botlhokwa thata, e gatelela tirisanommogo ya setlhopha go na le go tlhaloganya ditiro tsa motho a le esi.
Badiri ba tshwanetse go bona tumelelo ya babusi ba bone gantsi mo ditshwetso tse ba di dirang, mme go solofetswe gore ba ikobele ditaelo tsa babusi ba bone ntle le dipotso.
