Ɛsiane sɛ nnipa ntaa nte nkyɛnkyɛn, na hann a ɛsɛe nsoromma hwɛbea ntaa nyɛ ɔhaw nti, wobɛtumi nso anya nsoromma no ho anigye.
Japan adwumayɛ ho amammerɛ mu no, mpanyin-ne-nkumaa ntam nsonsonoe da adi kɛse na nneɛma yɛ nhyehyɛe pa ara sen sɛnea Atɔeɛ famfoɔ abrabɔ teɛ.
Atadeɛ tenten yɛ adwuma ho ntadeɛ a agye din, na adwumayɛfoɔ frɛ wɔn ho wɔn ho abusuabɔ din anaa adwuma din.
Asomdwoeɛ wɔ adwumam ho hia pa ara, ɛnam so de adwene si adwumakuo mmɔdenbɔ so mmom sen sɛ yɛbɛkamfo ankorankoro nneɛma a wɔatumi ayɛ.
Mpanyimfo taa hwehwɛ sɛ adwumayɛfo nya wɔn mpanyimfo penee wɔ gyinae biara a wobesi ho, na wɔhwɛ kwan sɛ wobetie wɔn mpanyimfo akwankyerɛ a wɔremmisa ho asɛm.
