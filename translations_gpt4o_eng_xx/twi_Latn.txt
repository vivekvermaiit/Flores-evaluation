Adwumayɛfoɔ no gyina hɔ sɛ wobɛhyehyɛ wɔn mpanyimfoɔ kɔkɔbɔ wɔ nneyɛeɛ biara a wɔyɛ no ho, na wɔyɛ wɔn mpanyimfoɔ ahyɛdeɛ no a, wɔmpɛ sɛ wɔhwehwɛ nsɛmmisa biara.
