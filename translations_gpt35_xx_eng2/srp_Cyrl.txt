This new environment has different resources and different opponents, so the new population will have to have different characteristics or adapt to be a stronger opponent than was previously necessary.
The original population has not changed at all, so they still need the same adaptations as before.
Over time, as the new population begins to adapt to its new environment, it increasingly becomes less similar to the other population.
