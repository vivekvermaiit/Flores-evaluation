This new environment has different resources and different competitors, so the new population must have different characteristics or adapt in order to be a stronger competitor than it used to be necessary before.
The original population has not changed at all, they still need the same adaptations.
Over time, as the new population begins to adapt to the new environment, it will increasingly resemble the other population less.
Finally, after several thousand or perhaps a million years, the two populations will be so different from each other that they won't even be able to be called the same species.
